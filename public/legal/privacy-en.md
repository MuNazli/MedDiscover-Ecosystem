# Privacy Policy

**MedDiscover GmbH**  
Date: December 2024  
Version: 1.0.0

## 1. Data Controller

Responsible for data processing:

MedDiscover GmbH  
Email: datenschutz@meddiscover.de

## 2. Collection and Processing of Personal Data

### 2.1 When Using Our Website

When using our website, the following data is collected:
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system used
- Date and time of access

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest)

### 2.2 When Submitting Inquiries via Contact Form

When you submit an inquiry, we collect:
- First and last name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Desired treatment type
- Your message
- Language preference
- IP address and consent timestamp

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(a) and (b) GDPR (consent and contract performance)

### 2.3 Health Data

Information about desired treatments may be considered health data under Art. 9 GDPR. Processing is based on your explicit consent (Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR).

## 3. Purposes of Processing

We process your data for the following purposes:
- Processing your inquiry
- Contacting you by email or phone
- Facilitating contact with partner clinics
- Improving our services

## 4. Data Sharing

Your data is only shared with:
- Partner clinics in Turkey (only with your consent)
- IT service providers (data processing agreement)
- Authorities (when legally required)

## 5. Storage Period

Your data is stored for:
- Inquiry data: 2 years after last contact
- Contract data: 10 years (legal retention obligation)
- After expiration, data is deleted or anonymized

## 6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:
- **Information** (Art. 15): Information about your stored data
- **Rectification** (Art. 16): Correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** (Art. 17): Deletion of your data
- **Restriction** (Art. 18): Restriction of processing
- **Data portability** (Art. 20): Receive your data in machine-readable format
- **Objection** (Art. 21): Object to processing
- **Withdrawal** (Art. 7(3)): Withdraw your consent

## 7. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures:
- SSL/TLS encryption
- Access control
- Regular security updates

## 8. Cookies

We only use technically necessary cookies for:
- Session management
- Security features

## 9. Right to Complain

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.

## 10. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated. The current version is always available on our website.
